# 🛍️ Myntra Fashion Product Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard developed using the Myntra Fashion Product Dataset. The dashboard analyzes product pricing, brand performance, customer ratings, and product distribution through interactive visualizations and business insights.

- Handled missing values
- Converted data types

## 🔑 Key Insights

- The dashboard analyzes **7,705 fashion products** across multiple brands available on Myntra.
- Products are categorized into **Budget, Affordable, Mid Range, Premium, and Luxury** price bands to understand pricing distribution.
- A few brands contribute a significant share of the total product catalog, while many brands have a relatively smaller presence.
- Most products fall within the **Budget and Affordable** price segments, indicating a strong focus on value-driven fashion.
- Customer ratings are generally positive, with the majority of rated products falling into the **Good**, **Very Good**, and **Excellent** categories.
- Brand comparison reveals noticeable differences in **average selling price** and **customer ratings**, helping identify premium and high-performing brands.
- The dashboard enables interactive exploration of product performance using **Brand**, **Price Band**, and **Rating Band** filters.
- The report provides actionable insights into **pricing strategy**, **brand performance**, and **customer satisfaction**, supporting data-driven business decisions.
